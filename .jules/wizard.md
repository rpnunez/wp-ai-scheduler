## 2024-05-23 - Client-side Search for Templates
**Learning:** `templates.php` loads all records at once, but lacked search functionality, forcing users to scan manually.

## 2024-05-24 - Planner "Copy Selected" and Toolbar
**Learning:** The Planner lacked intermediate actions; users could generate topics but not easily export them without scheduling.
**Action:** Added a cohesive "Toolbar" with "Copy Selected" and "Clear List" actions, providing immediate utility and better list management.
