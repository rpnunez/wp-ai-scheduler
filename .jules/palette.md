## 2024-05-22 - Editable AI Suggestions
**Learning:** Users often want to tweak AI-generated content slightly (e.g., fixing a year, adding a specific nuance) rather than accepting or rejecting it in a binary way. Providing inline editing immediately after generation reduces the friction of "generate -> copy -> edit -> paste".
**Action:** When displaying AI-generated lists or suggestions that will be processed further, default to editable inputs instead of static text.
