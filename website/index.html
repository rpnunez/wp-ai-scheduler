About

A WordPress plugin built for repeatability: schedule templates, generate structured drafts, route posts through review, and keep a clear audit trail of automation and AI activity.

What you get Admin-first
  • Template scheduling with variables, prompts, and cadence controls.
  • Author/topic workflows for repeatable editorial operations.
  • Research + review so your team stays in control of quality.
  • Recovery safeguards for partial generations and cron interruptions.

Features

Everything you need to generate content responsibly: scheduling, templating, review flows, and the operational guardrails that make automation dependable.

Template-driven generation

Use reusable templates with built-in variables, AI variables, and structured prompt sections to keep output consistent.

Author/topic workflows

Organize topics per author, schedule generation runs, and keep topic coverage aligned with your strategy.

Review-ready drafts

Route generated posts to review, add feedback loops, and keep humans in the loop before anything publishes.

Reliable scheduling

WordPress cron automation with recovery safeguards that detect partial generations and reconcile state.

Composable structures

Build reusable article structures and prompt sections, then plug them into templates for repeatable outputs.

History & observability

Track runs, retries, and outcomes with clear history logs—perfect for teams and regulated workflows.
