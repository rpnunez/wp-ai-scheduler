# Trending Topics Research

## Overview

The Trending Topics Research feature uses AI to automatically discover what's currently trending in your niche, helping you create timely and relevant content without the manual effort of topic brainstorming.

## How It Works

1. **Input**: You provide a niche (e.g., "AI Technology") and optional keywords.
2. **Analysis**: The AI analyzes current trends, search patterns, and seasonal relevance.
3. **Scoring**: Each topic is scored from 1-100 based on its potential impact.
4. **Output**: A list of high-potential blog post titles with reasoning and keywords.

## Usage Guide

### 1. Accessing the Tool
Navigate to `AI Post Scheduler → Trending Topics` in your WordPress admin dashboard.

### 2. Running a Research Session
1. In the "New Research" panel, enter your **Niche**.
2. (Optional) Add **Focus Keywords** to narrow down the results.
3. Select the **Number of Topics** to generate (default: 10).
4. Click **Research Trending Topics**.

### 3. Understanding Results
Results are displayed immediately and saved to your library. Each result includes:
- **Topic Title**: The suggested headline.
- **Score**: A relevance score (0-100).
  - **90-100**: High potential, very timely.
  - **70-89**: Good potential, relevant.
  - **<70**: Lower potential, may be niche or less timely.
- **Reason**: Why this topic is trending now.
- **Keywords**: SEO keywords associated with the topic.

## Scoring System

The scoring algorithm considers several factors:
- **Temporal Relevance**: Is it related to current events or the current year?
- **Seasonality**: Does it match the current season or upcoming holidays?
- **Search Volume**: Is there likely high search interest?
- **Content Gap**: Is there an opportunity for unique content?
- **Evergreen Value**: Will it remain relevant over time?

## Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: "Email Marketing for SaaS" yields better results than just "Marketing".
- **Use Keywords**: Guide the AI with specific keywords like "2024", "tutorial", or "strategy".
- **Regular Research**: Trends change fast. Run research weekly for dynamic niches.
- **Mix It Up**: Combine trending topics with evergreen content for a balanced strategy.
