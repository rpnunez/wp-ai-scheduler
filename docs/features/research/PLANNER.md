# Content Planner

## Overview

The Content Planner is a visual tool for scheduling your researched topics. It bridges the gap between idea generation (Research) and content creation (Post Generation).

## Features

### 1. Visual Schedule
See your upcoming content schedule at a glance. The planner displays scheduled posts in a calendar or list view.

### 2. Scheduling from Research
You can schedule topics directly from the Trending Topics Library:
1. Select one or more topics.
2. Click **Schedule Topics**.
3. Choose a **Template** (e.g., "Standard Blog Post").
4. Set a **Start Date** and **Frequency** (e.g., "Daily starting tomorrow").
5. The system automatically creates schedule entries for each topic.

### 3. Integration with Templates
The Planner works with your existing Templates. When you schedule a topic, you assign it to a template. The template defines *how* the post is generated (structure, length, style), while the topic defines *what* the post is about.

## Workflow

1. **Research**: Generate ideas in the Trending Topics tool.
2. **Select**: Choose the best ideas from the Library.
3. **Configure**: Assign a template and schedule in the Planner.
4. **Automate**: The system will automatically generate posts based on this schedule.

## Tips

- **Buffer Time**: Schedule posts a few days in advance to allow for review.
- **Variety**: Use different templates for different types of topics (e.g., "How-to" vs. "News").
- **Consistency**: Use the frequency setting to maintain a consistent publishing cadence.
